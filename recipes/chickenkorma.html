Chicken Korma

Korma is a dish originating in South Asia, consisting of meat or vegetables braised with yogurt (dahi), water or stock, and spices to produce a thick sauce or gravy.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Onion
  • Korma paste
  • Rice
  • Garlic
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Seal the chicken by frying it in oil
  2. Throw the rest of the ingredients in the pan and mix
  3. Add chicken stock and leave to simmer
  4. Leave for two hours
  5. Serve with rice or naan bread
