Cottage Pie

Cottage pie is a savoury dish of cooked minced meat topped with mashed potato and baked. The meat used may be either previously cooked or freshly minced. The usual meats are beef or lamb.

Ingredients

  • Minced lamb
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Beef stock
  • Grated cheese

Steps

  1. Boil and mash up the potatoes
  2. Fry the mince in a pan with chopped carrots
  3. Put the mince and carrots into a casserole dish
  4. Top with the mashed potatoes
  5. Bake in the oven for half an hour
