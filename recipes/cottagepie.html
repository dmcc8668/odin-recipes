Cottage Pie

Cottage pie is a savoury dish of cooked minced meat topped with mashed potato and baked. The meat used may be either previously cooked or freshly minced. The usual meats are beef or lamb.

Ingredients

  • Minced lamb
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Beef stock
  • Grated cheese
