Ulster Fry

An Ulster Fry is a substantial cooked breakfast meal, often served in Northern Ireland, that typically includes back bacon, sausages, eggs, black pudding, baked beans, some form of potato, tomatoes, mushrooms, toast, and a beverage such as coffee or tea.

Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Sausages
  • Beans
  • Mushrooms
  • Toast
  • Black pudding
